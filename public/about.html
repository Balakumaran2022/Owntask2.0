iEyal Solutions builds focused, practical products. We don't build bloated enterprise suites with features no one uses. We build tools that solve real problems, work reliably at scale, and feel good to use every day.

Why We Built ownTask

ownTask was born from a real frustration: managing customer-facing tasks inside support workflows with no SLA visibility, no recurring automation, and no accountability trail. We built ownTask to fix that — and kept building until it was the most complete task management platform we'd ever seen for business teams.

Mission & Vision

Mission

To give every business team the visibility, accountability, and automation they need to do their best work.

Vision

A world where no task is missed, no SLA is breached silently, and no team member is left without clarity on what they own.

Our Core Values

Four pillars that define every line of code we ship.

Simplicity

Powerful features shouldn't require a manual. Everything in ownTask has an obvious purpose and a clear outcome.

Accountability

The audit trail, the SLA system, the approval workflow — these aren't enterprise extras. They're the core. Every action is traceable.

Reliability

Built for scale. Multi-tenant from day one. Role-based access at every level. Your data stays yours.

Team-First

We build for the people doing the work, not just the people watching the dashboards. Round-robin, push notifications, reminders — all for the doers.
